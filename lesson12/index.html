Bila Tserkva athletes won the National Wrestling Championships

The championship of Ukraine in Greco-Roman wrestling among cadets took place in Kyiv, April 4, 2021, which gathered more than 250 participants from all over our country.Lyceum students of the Municipal Institution of the Kyiv Regional Council "Kyiv Regional Lyceum-Boarding School of Physical Culture and Sports" as part of the Kyiv region took part in these competitions and showed the following results:

The winner of the championship was Yatsenko Roman in the weight category of 51 kg and fulfilled the standard of the master of sports of Ukraine in Greco-Roman wrestling.

Khutko Artem won silver medals in the weight category of 71 kg and Yashkin Kyrylo in the weight category of 80 kg, winning the right to represent Ukraine at the world championship.

Mohammad Arian won a bronze medal in the 60 kg weight category.

The opening of McDonald's restaurant in Bila Tserkva

This was announced today, March 30, during the press conference "Results 2020 and investments in 2021. Key Directions and Regional Development ”organized by McDonald’s Ukraine.

In 2021, McDonald's accelerates development by focusing on investing in local communities. For the first time, restaurants will open in such cities as Ivano-Frankivsk, Bila Tserkva, Lutsk and Khmelnytsky. The company is ready to invest in other cities of Ukraine, where it is not yet represented.
