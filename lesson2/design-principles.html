Visual Hierarchy

Folkways

folkways.today

The first thing that viewers see when visiting this site is the traditional Slavic dish called “Borsch”. The bright contrasting colors and large size of the picture immediately catches our attention. Then goes the header or the title of the page and the logo of the site. Last of all the visitor will see the explanation text in a small font and the menu bar.

Repetition

Cumorah Academy

cumorahacademy.org

Even though, the Cumorah Academy main page is very bright, colorful and functional it still looks very consistent. Proper repetitions of colors and fonts, as well as sizes, shades, shapes and navigation structure create the balanced picture and a sense of cohesive relationship between all the elements. The visitor can easily find whatever he is looking for and navigate the site.

White Space

WeCanSoft

wecansoft.com.ua

The desktop of WeCanSoft Company site demonstrates the White Space Design Principle very well. The reason is not in the white colors usage rather than in abundance of space in-between and around all of the elements placed on the page. It makes the site to look very neat, readable, focused and creates visual hierarchy.
