Audience

The target audience are local people and organizations whose productivity depends on the weather conditions or weather-dependent services. People of any age and social status, of different job positions and with different income. Useful authorized facts about events on the local or international level that may influence their daily activities or their business in long term perspective. Clients will be accessing the site and its content online on all classes of devices—large-wide screens (desktop/laptop), medium-screens (tablet), and small-screens (smartphones /smart-watches).

As the site will be designed for such a wide range of people, we will make sure a five-year-old will be able to navigate and find whatever he or she is looking for. The information we will present will be precise and concise. The interface will be consistent in colors, shapes and typography, people friendly in usage and nice to look at. The forecast we provide will be as accurate as possible so that people will be able rely on it and every day to come back. On the desktop will be a text holder where anyone can post a question, ask for assistance or leave their comments and feedback.

Personas

Agricultural Sector Workers. This large group of specialists consists of crop farmers, smallholder and industrialized farmers, livestock herders, fishermen and those involved in the agricultural value chain, ranging from seed salesmen to livestock buyers. This target group depends on the weather forecast and climate information. Accurate information of weather changes can save lives, increase productivity, and reduce risks.

Community service workers such as builders and repairmen socialists, or utility workers who take care of pipes, roads, heating systems, lights etc. A lot of there job is happening outside of buildings so weather conditions affect a lot on their productivity and safety.

Scenarios

  1. Farmer

    Spring has started and a farmer is planning on planting different kinds of potatoes and some other vegetables. He is going to sell them in the market. Though in a condition that weather temperature will be lower than -3 degrees Celsius. If the temperature will suddenly fall down the harvest will not bring much good.

  2. Travelers

    A group of young people are planning a trip to another country. They have no idea what the weather is like over there, is it cold or worm, how humid and strong is the wind and what is the water temperature or precipitations. In order to be ready and to pick more weather stable days to travel. For all this information they can monitor at the Hot&Cold Weather Center site. In addition, they might check the latest news for the place they are going to visit.

  3. Parents

    One family wants to go for a hike and do a picnic, the clouds seems to be coming and they are not sure if that is good time to go. They open the Weather Forecast and see that the humidity is very low, so the chance that the rain is going to come is just tiny.

  4. Community Service Workers

    A repairman has received a call to check and repair head-lights in one local street. He is going t use different electric and metal equipment, as well as ladder. That’s a high-risk job and he needs to be sure the weather conditions will allow him to work.

  5. Filmmakers

    Let’s say there is movie filmed and one of the scenes is to be in rain or thunderstorm, filmmakers want it to be natural, so they constantly are checking the Weather Sites for an opportunity to get this part of movie done.
