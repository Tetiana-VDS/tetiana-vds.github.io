Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of our website is to provide local people and organizations with the most accurate prediction of what the weather will be like in the near future in order to reduce weather-related losses and enhance the benefits. As well as our site “Hot & Cold Local Weather Center” will familiarize folks with the top political and social news of their country, and the main events in the world, including public health and safety, economics and quality of life. The functional, easy in use and navigation site will make the users experience meaningful and relevant.

Audience

The target audience are local people and organizations whose productivity depends on the weather conditions or weather-dependent services. People of any age and social status, of different job positions and with different income. Useful authorized facts about events on the local or international level that may influence their daily activities or their business in long term perspective. Clients will be accessing the site and its content online on all classes of devices—large-wide screens (desktop/laptop), medium-screens (tablet), and small-screens (smartphones /smart-watches).

As the site will be designed for such a wide range of people, we will make sure a five-year-old will be able to navigate and find whatever he or she is looking for. The information we will present will be precise and concise. The interface will be consistent in colors, shapes and typography, people friendly in usage and nice to look at. The forecast we provide will be as accurate as possible so that people will be able rely on it and every day to come back. On the desktop will be a text holder where anyone can post a question, ask for assistance or leave their comments and feedback.

Typography

Heading Font: "Noto Serif", serif;

To my mind Noto Serif' Font will look visually nice, and will grab people attention as a Heading Font for the Weather Forecast Website as this. It is easy to read and it contrast very with the text font.

Paragraph Font: "Trebuchet MS", Helvetica, sans-serif.

For the main text Font I have chosen "Trebuchet MS", Helvetica as it is casual, readable and easy-comprehendible.
